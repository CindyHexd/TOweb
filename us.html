Overview

The United States program offers applicants the opportunity to immerse themselves in the culture, explore educational opportunities, and develop global leadership skills.

Key Features:
  • Focus on academic and leadership development.
  • Collaboration with peers from around the world.
  • Workshops on U.S. history, culture, and innovation.

Requirements

To apply, you must meet the following requirements:

  • Be a high school student (grades 10-12).
  • Demonstrate strong academic and leadership skills.
  • Show interest in cross-cultural exchange.

How to Apply

Follow these steps to apply:

  1. Fill out the online application form.
  2. Submit your academic transcripts and a personal statement.
  3. Provide two letters of recommendation from teachers or mentors.
